A$AP Rocky

ABOUT

A$AP Rocky is an American Rapper. His real name is Rakim Athelaston Mayers. He was born on October 3rd 1988, in Harlem. He has won many awards including BET Awards,BET Hip Hop Awards and many more.

He first learned to rap at the age of 9 after his older brother taught him. He dicided to take his rapping seriouly after his brothers death. He joined the A$AP Mob crew in 2007. In 2011 he released his single "Purple Swag", which brought him a lot of attention from record labels.
